### Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Do not click on suspicious links or emails  
- Always verify website URLs before entering details  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Never share OTP or banking details with anyone  
- Regularly update your passwords  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Monitor bank transactions regularly  

📌 Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
