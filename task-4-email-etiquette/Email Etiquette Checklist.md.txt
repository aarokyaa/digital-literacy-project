### Email Etiquette Checklist

#### Do’s
- Use a clear and relevant subject line  
- Start with a polite greeting (Dear Sir/Madam, Dear Professor, etc.)  
- Keep the message clear, concise, and respectful  
- Use proper grammar and correct spelling  
- End with a professional closing (Sincerely, Regards, etc.)  
- Include your name and details at the end  

#### Don’ts
- Do not use informal language or slang  
- Avoid emails without a subject line  
- Do not write in all capital letters  
- Avoid sending long or unclear messages  
- Do not forget to proofread before sending
